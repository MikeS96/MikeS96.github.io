Miguel Saavedra-Ruiz

I am a a Postgraduate Diploma student in Artificial Intelligence at Universidad Autonoma de Occidente (UAO) and a R&D Robotics Software Engineer at Romero Cano Igenieria (RCI) where I work on Robotics vision, Multi-modal sensor fusion, AI and UAVs.

Previously, I obtained a BEng degree as Mechatronics Engineering with emphasis in Robotics from the UAO. There, I had the chance to work under the supervision of Victor Romero-Cano on different areas such as object detection and tracking, mobile robotics, sensor data fusion and deep neural networks.

My undergraduate degree project was entitled "Autonomous landing system for an unmanned aerial vehicle on a terrestrial vehicle" and consisted in the development of the vision and control pipelines to autonomously land a UAV on a ground vehicle. The university granted me the Academic Excellence Award for the highest GPA obtained in the Engineering department.

I am looking for an exciting MSc or MASc opportunity in Robotics vision, AI or RL where I can contribute into these fascinating fields..

Research

I am broadly interested in the areas of Robotics vision, Machine Learning, Self-Driving vehicles, Deep neural networks, Reinforcement Learning and SLAM. Much of my research is motivated in how intelligent systems can be endowed with environmental awareness and start perceiving its surrounding in an accurate and reliable way, enhancing their decision-making capabilities.
